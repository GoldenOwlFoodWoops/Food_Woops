About us

Our passion is simple: to share with our patrons the flavors of India we know and love—the family-run food shops, the off-the-beaten-track gems of daily Indian streets, and the ʻin the knowʼ artisanal offerings that we’ve discovered, one at a time. Food Woops was established in 2016 as the local boutique travel agency specialized in organizing private culinary tours and activities across India for travelers and food professionals looking for a genuine culinary travel experience in India. Founded by a team of professionals with extensive background in the fine food sector, Food Woops is the first culinary travel agency of its type in India. Whether on business or vacation, thinking on expanding your knowledge of India´s exceptional gastronomic offer, or just having a group of people interested in a tour to the culinary treasures of India, we put at your disposal our net of contacts and know-how.
